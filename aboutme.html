About Me

Hello, my name is Hady.

I am passionate about technology, creativity, and building digital solutions that solve real-world problems. I design and develop websites that are clean, responsive, and user-friendly, focusing on creating digital experiences that are both functional and visually appealing.

I am also the founder of NAVO, a transport business focused on providing premier driver-on-demand service, connecting individuals and organizations with professional, licensed drivers who drive your car. We provide trusted drivers so you can focus on your schedule, business, or events without worrying about getting home safely. Through NAVO, I apply my creativity, problem-solving skills, and entrepreneurial mindset to build meaningful experiences for customers.

My goal is to combine technology and business to create modern digital platforms that help businesses grow and connect with their audience.
