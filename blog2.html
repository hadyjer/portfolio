Vehicle Management for Owners

Turn Your Vehicle Into a Reliable Source of Income In Uganda, many vehicle owners generate income by leasing their vehicles to drivers who make regular weekly or monthly payments. These vehicles are commonly used for commercial transport, such as moving goods with trucks like Canters. However, managing drivers, tracking payments, and ensuring proper vehicle use can be time-consuming—especially for busy professionals and business owners. This is where NAVO comes in.

How NAVO Helps Vehicle Owners NAVO provides professional vehicle management services that allow owners to earn from their vehicles without dealing with the daily operational stress. We help owners by: Identifying and assigning reliable drivers Monitoring vehicle usage and performance Managing weekly or monthly payment collections Ensuring accountability and responsible vehicle handling Providing updates and communication to vehicle owners With NAVO, your vehicle continues to generate income while we handle the management.
